About Us

Comedian is a comedy club that has been in business for over 20 years. We have hosted some of the best comedians in the world and have a reputation for providing a great night out. Our club is located in the heart of the city and is easily accessible by public transport. We have a fully stocked bar and a great selection of food to choose from. So come on down and have a laugh with us!
