Alice

Alice is the curious, imaginative, and adventurous protagonist of the story. She is a young girl who stumbles into Wonderland after following a White Rabbit down a rabbit hole. Throughout her journey, Alice encounters strange characters and puzzling situations, testing her logic and sense of reality. Her curiosity and bravery drive her to explore Wonderland’s eccentricities, though she often finds herself frustrated by its nonsensical rules. By the end, she learns valuable lessons about identity, independence, and resilience.

The Mad Hatter

TThe Mad Hatter is a lively and eccentric character who hosts a never-ending tea party with the March Hare and the Dormouse. Known for his nonsensical riddles and paradoxes, he frustrates Alice with his strange questions and illogical conversation. He represents the playful, chaotic side of Wonderland, where logic and reason are constantly challenged.
